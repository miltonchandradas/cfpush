APP & SERVICE STATUS:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
cf apps
cf app myawesomephp
cf app myawesomenode

cf services
cf service myawesomehana-db

cf env myawesomenode

cf buildpacks


SETUP:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
cf create-service hana hdi-shared myawesomehana-db
cf push
cf scale myawesomephp -i 3


CLEANUP:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
cf delete -r -f myawesomenode
cf delete -r -f myawesomephp

cf delete-service myawesomehana-db -f
